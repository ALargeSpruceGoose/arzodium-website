About

Arzodium is an up-and-coming 2-D animation and written story. It derives its name from the fictional continent the events take place on.

Join five warriors (brave or otherwise) as they embark on a great adventure through the realm in order to stop the forces of darkness that threaten their homes. They will meet new enemies, find new allies, and uncover an ancient plan a thousand years in the making.

Can they save Arzodium?
